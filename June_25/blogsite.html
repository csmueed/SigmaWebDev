Basic HTML Tags

HTML is made of elements that structure your webpage. Some of the most commonly used tags include: Headings (h1 to h6) to define different levels of titles Paragraphs to add blocks of text Line breaks and horizontal rules to organize content visually Bold and italic tags to highlight important words These basic elements are like the building blocks of every webpage.
